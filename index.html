About Me

Research Background & Experience

Research Focus

I'm a postdoctoral researcher specializing in deep learning. My research focuses on addressing challenging problems in accelerating DNN models with low-precision quantization and pruning techniques.

Currently, I'm working at RIKEN Center for Computer Science, where I am a member of the Advanced AI Device Development Unit leaded by Prof. Kentaro Sano to develop novel AI accelerators that push the boundaries of computational efficiency.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Osaka in 2025, where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Fumihiko Ino on accelerating DNN models on GPUs and developing innovative optimization strategies.
